About Us

It’s the combination of acquiring, developing and managing a diversified portfolio of quality properties and doing what’s right by our tenants, people and communities that allows us to consistently deliver value to our investors.

An argosy is a flotilla of ships. It’s strength arises from the sum of its parts. And that’s why it’s an apt name for us as well.

Our strength comes from a balanced approach across our stakeholders and our properties.

We believe in doing the right thing and doing things right. Treating our shareholders, tenants, employees and supply partners equally well lays the foundations for a strong company.

As one of New Zealand’s leading listed property companies, we own a diversified portfolio of industrial, office and retail properties predominately in Auckland and Wellington, with a modest tenant-driven exposure to other parts of New Zealand.

Our tenants span a wide variety of industries. Each one has unique spacial requirements but whatever they need, we always focus on delivering safe and workable environments that allow their businesses to prosper.

We are listed on the New Zealand Stock Exchange under the ticker code ARG. With more than 9,000 shareholders, it’s their money and on-going support allows us to keep getting stronger.
